Move Free like a Bird

iKiwi Sure

is a platform that collaborates data from our Smart Devices into complex social graph calculations to track the health of your favourite social place at real time. This means you would be aware of the historic health of the staff of that place, and even the health of the people who visited it in last 15 days. So you can visit your social life at much ease !

Being a Social Kiwi

iKiwi tells you the health Risks you may have from the People you meet and the Places you go.

Be it CORONA or Beyond, we just cannot stay in lockdown forever. We have developed iKiwi keeping the Kiwi bird as our inspiration for this ambitious project. Kiwi Bird is a selectively social creature. We have developed a robust artificially intelligent backend data center that leverages the power of Data analytics of our complex social interactions.

1. Get Instant Alerts for any risks from any person keeping ill health.

2. Get Notified if any person you crossed paths with recently starts showing symptoms of an Infection.

3. Get Alerts of Hotspots/places to avoid while on move.

So we ll take all the stressful part of tracking your social health graph. Now you can imagine going to club again with all healthy people around. Or maybe a Coffee Date with that Hot Profile from Tindrr, because he has a good health score and is very handsome too. Possibilities are endless when you are aware of any risks in your social Circle. P.S. The App ensures all data privacy concerns.
