iKiwi Sure is a platform that collaborates data from our Smart Devices into complex social graph calculations to track the health of your favourite social place at real time. This means you would be aware of the historic health of the staff of that place, and even the health of the people who visited it in last 15 days. So you can visit your social life at much ease !



How it Works

How it Works

Being a Social Kiwi

Alerts you for any Risk of Infection around you from the People you cross paths and the Places you visit; Covid-19 or beyond !


In this Post Covid era, we just cannot stay in lockdown forever. Kiwi bird, being a selectively social creature, was our inspiration for this ambitious project. We have developed a robust artificially intelligent backend data center that leverages the power of Data analytics of our complex social interactions.

So this is how it works


Preorder your iKiwi Smart watch to join the safe world - It monitors your health continously including your body temprature, Heart rate and many more Smart features.


Our system calculates your health scores based on 22+ complex parameters that include the historic health of the People you get in touch with, the places you visit, your last 15 days health graph and more.


Use your iKiwi APP(Coming soon) for your health score, and for health score of the social places around you. Find the places you know has a healthy staff & even the people who visited it were keeping good health score too. See the realtime score of the nearest SPA or Movie theater you wish to go.


You get instant Alerts for any unsafe location, for an infected person close by, and if any person you crossed paths days ago starts showing symptoms. Be always aware of what is safe for you.

So we do all the stressful part of tracking your social health graph. Now you can imagine going to club again with all healthy people around. Or maybe a Coffee Date with that Hot Profile from Tindrr, because he has a good health score and is very handsome too. Possibilities are endless when you are aware of any risks in your social Circle.
P.S. The App ensures that your data is kept private.
